# Notion AI Pricing Plans

## Overview

Notion offers flexible pricing plans to accommodate users of all sizes, from individual users to large enterprises. Each plan includes access to Notion AI features with varying capabilities and usage limits.

## Available Plans

### Free Plan

**Features:**
- Basic Notion workspace
- Limited AI monthly credits (50 credits/month)
- Community support
- Basic templates

**Use Cases:**
- Personal projects
- Small teams evaluating Notion
- Light documentation needs

**Pricing:** Free

### Plus Plan

**Features:**
- Enhanced workspace capabilities
- 500 AI credits per month
- Priority email support
- Advanced templates and integrations
- Guest access

**Use Cases:**
- Small teams (up to 5-10 members)
- Active documentation projects
- Regular AI assistance needs

**Pricing:** $10 per member per month (billed annually)

### Business Plan

**Features:**
- Team collaboration features
- 5,000 AI credits per month
- Priority support (24-hour response)
- Advanced security and permissions
- SSO (Single Sign-On)
- Bulk user management

**Use Cases:**
- Growing teams (10-50+ members)
- Enterprise documentation
- Heavy AI usage
- Advanced compliance requirements

**Pricing:** $20 per member per month (billed annually)

### Enterprise Plan

**Features:**
- Unlimited AI credits
- Dedicated support team
- Advanced security features
- Custom integrations
- Advanced admin controls
- SAML 2.0 SSO
- Audit logs
- SLA guarantees

**Use Cases:**
- Large organizations (100+ members)
- Mission-critical applications
- Complex compliance requirements
- Custom needs

**Pricing:** Custom quote

## AI Credits Explained

### What are AI Credits?
AI credits are a measure of AI usage. Each AI operation consumes credits based on complexity.

### Credit Costs
- Simple queries: 1-5 credits
- Complex analysis: 5-20 credits
- Large document processing: 10-50 credits

## FAQ

**Q: Can I use Notion AI across multiple workspaces?**
A: Yes, on Business and Enterprise plans.

**Q: Do unused credits expire?**
A: Credits expire 12 months after purchase.
