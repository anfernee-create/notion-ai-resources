# Notion AI Pricing Plans

> **Last Verified: April 2026** | For the most current pricing, always check [notion.so/pricing](https://www.notion.so/pricing)

## Overview

Notion AI is included as part of Notion's paid plans — it is not sold as a separate add-on. All paid plan members get full access to Notion AI features within their plan. The Free plan has limited AI access.

> **Important:** Pricing details change over time. The figures below reflect publicly available information as of early 2026. Always verify current pricing on Notion's official website before making purchasing decisions.

---

## Available Plans

### Free Plan

The Free plan provides limited access to Notion AI to let you try the features before committing to a paid plan.

**What's included:**
- Basic Notion workspace (unlimited blocks for personal use)
- Limited Notion AI usage (trial responses to explore features)
- Access to Notion AI on pages, databases, and documents
- Community support

**Best for:** Individuals evaluating Notion, students with light needs, personal projects

**Pricing:** Free forever

---

### Plus Plan

The Plus plan is designed for individuals and small teams who use Notion daily.

**What's included:**
- Everything in Free
- Full Notion AI access — unlimited responses for all AI features
- Unlimited file uploads
- 30-day page history
- Guest access (up to 100 guests)
- Priority support

**Best for:** Freelancers, solopreneurs, small teams of 2-10 members

**Pricing:** Approximately $10/member/month (billed annually) or $12/member/month (monthly)

---

### Business Plan

The Business plan is designed for growing teams with advanced collaboration needs.

**What's included:**
- Everything in Plus
- Full Notion AI access for all members
- SAML SSO (Single Sign-On)
- Advanced page analytics
- Bulk PDF export
- 90-day page history
- Advanced workspace security

**Best for:** Teams of 10-100+ members, businesses requiring SSO, teams with compliance requirements

**Pricing:** Approximately $15/member/month (billed annually) or $18/member/month (monthly)

---

### Enterprise Plan

The Enterprise plan is for large organizations with custom requirements.

**What's included:**
- Everything in Business
- Full Notion AI access for all members
- Dedicated customer success manager
- Custom security review
- Advanced audit logs
- SCIM provisioning
- Custom contracts and SLAs
- Advanced admin controls

**Best for:** Large organizations (100+ members), enterprise compliance requirements, mission-critical deployments

**Pricing:** Custom — contact Notion Sales for a quote

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is Notion AI included in paid plans or a separate purchase?**
A: As of 2025-2026, Notion AI is included with Plus, Business, and Enterprise plans at no additional cost. The previous credit-based add-on model was phased out.

**Q: Does the Free plan include Notion AI?**
A: Yes, but with limited trial usage. Once the trial is exhausted, you need a paid plan for continued AI access.

**Q: Is there a team discount?**
A: Notion offers volume discounts for large teams on Business and Enterprise plans. Contact their sales team for details.

**Q: Can I switch plans at any time?**
A: Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrading takes effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

**Q: Is there an education discount?**
A: Yes. Notion offers free Plus plans for verified students and educators. Visit notion.so/students for details.

---

## How to Upgrade

1. Go to **Settings** in your Notion workspace (click the gear icon)
2. Select **Plans** from the left sidebar
3. Choose your desired plan and click **Upgrade**
4. Enter billing information and confirm

For Enterprise plans, click **Contact Sales** instead.
