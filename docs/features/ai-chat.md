# Notion AI Chat & Q&A

Learn how to use Notion AI's conversational chat and question-answering capabilities.

## Overview

Notion AI Chat allows you to have conversations with your workspace data through natural language queries.

## Key Features

- Ask questions about your databases and pages
- Get immediate answers from your workspace
- Natural language understanding
- Multi-page search and analysis
- Export and save conversations

## Getting Started

1. Click "Ask AI" in any Notion page
2. Type your question in natural language
3. Notion AI will search and analyze your data
4. Review the generated answer
5. Ask follow-up questions

## Question Types

### Factual Queries
- "Who is the project lead?"
- "What's our Q3 budget?"
- "List all overdue tasks"

### Analytical Questions
- "What trends do you see in our data?"
- "Which customers have the highest engagement?"
- "Summarize the key insights"

### Recommendation Requests
- "Which option should we choose?"
- "What's the best approach?"
- "Recommend improvements"

## Tips for Better Results

- Be specific and clear
- Provide context in your question
- Reference specific databases or pages
- Ask follow-ups for clarification

## Limitations

- Works within workspace boundaries
- Limited by token context window
- Requires well-structured data
