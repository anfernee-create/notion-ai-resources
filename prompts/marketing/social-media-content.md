# Social Media Content Marketing Prompts for Notion AI

## LinkedIn Post Creator

### Prompt:
You are an expert LinkedIn content strategist. Create a compelling LinkedIn post about [TOPIC] that:
- Opens with a compelling hook or question
- Provides 3-5 key insights or takeaways
- Includes relevant metrics or data points
- Ends with a call-to-action that encourages engagement
- Uses professional but conversational tone
- Includes relevant hashtags
- Is optimized for LinkedIn's algorithm (between 150-250 words)

### Example Topics:
- AI automation in business processes
- Remote work productivity trends
- Industry-specific insights
- Career development advice
- Thought leadership perspectives

## Instagram Caption Generator

- Starts with an attention-grabbing opening line

- Customer success stories
- Educational tips and tricks
- Lifestyle and wellness content

## Twitter Thread Developer

### Prompt:
You are an expert Twitter strategist. Create a viral Twitter thread about [TOPIC] that:
- Opens with an intriguing first tweet that hooks readers
- Continues with 5-8 follow-up tweets building on each other
- Each tweet is under 280 characters
- Uses numbered format (1/, 2/, 3/, etc.)
- Includes data, quotes, or surprising insights
- Maintains consistent voice and pacing
- Ends with a clear takeaway or call-to-action
- Contains 1-2 relevant hashtags only

### Example Topics:
- Industry trends and predictions
- Productivity hacks
- Business lessons learned
- Technology insights
- Marketing strategies

## Email Subject Line Generator

### Prompt:
You are an email marketing expert. Generate 10 compelling subject lines for [EMAIL_TOPIC] that:
- Each is under 50 characters
- Creates curiosity or urgency without being clickbait
- Are personalization-friendly
- Avoid spam trigger words
- Resonate with audience: [TARGET_AUDIENCE]
- Include variety: questions, numbers, urgency, benefit-driven
- Test different emotional triggers

### Example Variations:
- Curiosity-driven
- FOMO-based (fear of missing out)
- Number-based
- Question-based
- Benefit-driven

## YouTube Thumbnail and Title Generator

### Prompt:
You are a YouTube optimization expert. Create 5 compelling thumbnail concepts and titles for a video about [VIDEO_TOPIC] that:

**Titles** should:
- Be 60 characters or less
- Include primary keyword
- Use power words
- Create curiosity gap
- Be clickable but honest

**Thumbnails** should:
- Include bold, readable text
- Use contrasting colors
- Feature clear facial expressions or action
- Stand out in the 150x150px size
- Avoid excessive text or clutter

### Example Topics:
- Tutorials and how-tos
- Reviews and comparisons
- Vlogs and storytelling
- Educational content
- Entertainment content
