# Key Insights Extraction Prompts

## Overview

These AI prompts help you extract meaningful insights, patterns, and strategic takeaways from data, documents, meetings, and conversations. Use them to identify what matters most and turn information into actionable intelligence.

## Meeting Insights

### Strategic Insights

**Prompt**: "Analyze this meeting transcript and identify the most important strategic insights. For each insight, provide:

[MEETING TRANSCRIPT]

1. **Key Insight**: What was learned or revealed
2. **Significance**: Why it matters
3. **Implications**: What this means for the business/project
4. **Recommended Actions**: What should be done about it
5. **Stakeholders**: Who needs to know

Prioritize insights by potential impact."

### Decision Points

**Prompt**: "Review these meeting notes and extract key decision points and the reasoning behind them:

[MEETING NOTES]

For each decision:
- What was decided
- Rationale and supporting evidence
- Alternative options considered
- Potential risks or concerns
- Success criteria
- Next steps

Highlight any unresolved decisions requiring follow-up."

### Pattern Recognition

**Prompt**: "Identify recurring themes, patterns, and trends across these meetings/discussions:

[MEETING SERIES]

Look for:
- Repeated concerns or blockers
- Consistent feedback themes
- Emerging opportunities
- Evolving priorities
- Communication gaps
- Areas of alignment or conflict

Provide evidence for each pattern and suggest implications."

## Data Analysis Insights

### Report Analysis

**Prompt**: "Extract the most critical insights from this report or analysis:

[REPORT/DATA]

**Key Findings:**
- What are the 3-5 most important discoveries?

**Trends:**
- What patterns or trends are evident?

**Anomalies:**
- What's unexpected or surprising?

**Business Impact:**
- How do these findings affect business goals?

**Recommendations:**
- What should be done based on this data?

Present insights in order of business priority."

### Comparative Analysis

**Prompt**: "Compare these two [reports/periods/approaches] and identify key insights:

**Source A:**
[CONTENT A]

**Source B:**
[CONTENT B]

Provide:
- Critical differences
- Significant improvements or declines
- Common themes
- Unexpected divergences
- Strategic implications
- Recommended focus areas

Highlight insights that drive decision-making."

### Performance Insights

**Prompt**: "Analyze this performance data and extract actionable insights:

[PERFORMANCE DATA]

Identify:
- Top performers: What's working exceptionally well
- Underperformers: What needs attention
- Opportunities: Where there's untapped potential
- Risks: What could become problematic
- Quick wins: Easy improvements with high impact
- Strategic priorities: Where to focus resources

Provide specific recommendations for each insight."

## Research & Document Insights

### Research Synthesis

**Prompt**: "Synthesize insights from this research or collection of documents:

[RESEARCH/DOCUMENTS]

Provide:
- **Core Findings**: The most important discoveries
- **Supporting Evidence**: Key data points or quotes
- **Contradictions**: Where sources disagree
- **Gaps**: What's missing or unclear
- **Practical Applications**: How to use these insights
- **Further Questions**: What still needs investigation

Organize by relevance to [specific goal/question]."

[COMPETITIVE DATA]

Identify:
- Competitor strengths and weaknesses
- Market opportunities
- Threats to our position
- Differentiators we can leverage
- Industry trends
- Strategic gaps in the market
- Recommended positioning

Prioritize insights that inform strategy."

Extract:

## Strategic Insights

### SWOT Analysis

**Prompt**: "Based on this information, provide a strategic SWOT analysis with actionable insights:

[CONTEXT/INFORMATION]

**Strengths:**
- Internal advantages
- Key capabilities
- Competitive differentiators

**Weaknesses:**
- Internal limitations
- Areas needing improvement
- Vulnerabilities

**Opportunities:**
- External favorable conditions
- Market gaps
- Growth potential

**Threats:**
- External challenges
- Competitive pressures
- Market risks

For each item, explain why it matters and what actions should be taken."

### Trend Analysis

**Prompt**: "Identify emerging trends and strategic insights from this information:

[MARKET/INDUSTRY DATA]

For each trend:
- Description and evidence
- Current stage (emerging, growing, mature, declining)
- Potential impact on our business
- Timeline for significance
- Required response
- Opportunity or threat assessment

Prioritize trends by urgency and impact."

### Risk & Opportunity Assessment

**Prompt**: "Analyze this situation/data for risks and opportunities:

[SITUATION/DATA]

**Risks:**
- What could go wrong
- Probability and impact
- Early warning signs
- Mitigation strategies

**Opportunities:**
- What could go right
- Potential value
- Requirements to capture
- Timeline to act

Provide actionable recommendations for both."

## Insight Synthesis

### Executive Summary

**Prompt**: "Create an executive summary of key insights from this information:

[DETAILED INFORMATION]

**Format:**
- **Top 3 Insights**: The most critical takeaways
- **So What**: Why each insight matters
- **Now What**: Recommended actions
- **Timeline**: When actions should be taken
- **Resources**: What's needed to execute

Keep it concise and action-oriented for senior leadership."

### Insight Connections

**Prompt**: "Analyze these separate insights and identify connections, patterns, and meta-insights:

**Individual Insights:**
[LIST OF INSIGHTS]

Identify:
- How insights relate to each other
- Overarching patterns or themes
- Contradictions that need resolution
- Combined implications
- Integrated recommendations
- Holistic strategic perspective

Provide a synthesized view that's greater than the sum of parts."

## Best Practices

1. **Look beyond the obvious** - Extract insights that aren't immediately apparent
2. **Context matters** - Consider business goals and constraints
3. **Prioritize ruthlessly** - Not all insights are equally important
4. **Be specific** - Vague insights aren't actionable
5. **Support with evidence** - Back insights with data or examples
6. **Connect to action** - Every insight should lead to a potential decision or action
7. **Challenge assumptions** - Question conventional thinking
8. **Consider stakeholders** - Different insights matter to different audiences
9. **Think long-term** - Balance immediate and strategic insights
10. **Validate insights** - Test conclusions against multiple sources
