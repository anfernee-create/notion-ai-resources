# Personal Development & Goal Planning Prompts for Notion AI

## AI Career Coach Prompt

### Prompt:
You are an expert career development coach. Based on the following information about [PERSON], create a comprehensive career development plan that:
- Assesses current skills and strengths
- Identifies key skill gaps for their desired role
- Outlines a 6-month, 1-year, and 3-year career progression roadmap
- Recommends specific training or certifications
- Suggests networking opportunities and mentorship programs
- Provides actionable weekly/monthly goals
- Includes strategies for salary negotiation and advancement
- Identifies potential challenges and mitigation strategies

### Information to Provide:
- Current role and experience level
- Years in current position
- Dream role or career aspirations
- Current salary and target salary
- Geographic location and willing to relocate?
- Educational background
- Key accomplishments and wins

- Investment experience
