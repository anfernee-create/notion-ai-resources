# Tone & Voice Adjustment Prompts

## Overview

This collection of AI prompts helps you adjust and refine the tone, voice, and emotional quality of written content. These prompts are designed to transform text to match different audiences, contexts, and communication goals.

## Tone Adjustment Prompts

### Formal to Casual

**Prompt**: "Transform this formal text into a casual, conversational tone while maintaining the core message and accuracy:

[TEXT]

Make it sound like you're talking to a friend, but keep it professional enough for a business audience."

### Professional Tone

**Prompt**: "Convert this text to a professional business tone. Remove any slang, casual language, or overly friendly phrasing. Make it appropriate for corporate communication, email, or formal documentation:

[TEXT]

Maintain clarity while adding polish and authority to the language."

### Friendly & Approachable

**Prompt**: "Rewrite this content to be warm, friendly, and approachable. Add personality while keeping the information intact. This should feel like it's coming from someone who genuinely cares about the reader:

[TEXT]

Include conversational elements but maintain clarity."

### Authoritative & Expert

**Prompt**: "Enhance this text to sound more authoritative and expert-level. Use precise terminology, confident language, and structured thinking. Position the writer as a knowledgeable authority:

[TEXT]

Provide 2 versions: one with technical depth and one accessible to professionals."

### Empathetic & Compassionate

**Prompt**: "Rewrite this to be more empathetic and emotionally intelligent. Show understanding of the reader's feelings and challenges. Make it feel supportive and caring:

[TEXT]

Maintain the factual content while adding emotional resonance."

### Neutral & Objective

**Prompt**: "Convert this text to a neutral, objective tone. Remove personal opinions, emotional language, emotional appeals, and bias. Make it sound factual and unbiased:

[TEXT]

Keep it informative without influencing reader emotions."

### Urgent & Action-Oriented

**Prompt**: "Make this content sound more urgent and action-oriented. Create a sense of importance and motivate immediate response. Use power words and compelling language:

[TEXT]

Maintain truthfulness while creating urgency."

### Encouraging & Motivational

**Prompt**: "Transform this text into an encouraging and motivational tone. Inspire confidence, highlight possibilities, and energize the reader. Make them feel capable and motivated:

[TEXT]

Use positive framing and empowering language."

## Brand Voice Prompts

### Match Brand Voice

**Prompt**: "Rewrite this in [Brand Name] voice and style. [Brand Description/Examples]. Maintain the message but align with the brand's tone, values, and communication style:

[TEXT]

Provide the adjusted version that sounds authentically [Brand]."

### Age/Demographic Adjustment

**Prompt**: "Adjust this text to appeal to [AGE GROUP/DEMOGRAPHIC]. Use language, references, and communication style that resonate with this audience:

[TEXT]

Make it relevant and engaging for [specific audience]."

## Best Practices

1. **Be specific about target audience** - Define who should connect with the adjusted tone
2. **Preserve core message** - Tone should change, not the fundamental information
3. **Test multiple versions** - Compare different tone adjustments to find the best fit
4. **Consider context** - The same message may need different tones for different channels
5. **Maintain authenticity** - Don't change tone so much that it feels inauthentic to your brand
6. **Check for consistency** - Ensure tone adjustment aligns with your overall communication strategy
7. **Get feedback** - Have target audience members review the tone-adjusted content
