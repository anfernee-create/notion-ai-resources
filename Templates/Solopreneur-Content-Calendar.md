# Solopreneur Content Calendar Template

A comprehensive Notion template for managing your weekly content calendar as a solopreneur.

## Template Structure

### 1. Weekly Overview Dashboard
- **Week at a Glance**: Visual calendar showing all scheduled content
- **Content Pipeline Status**: Track content in different stages (Ideation, In Progress, Ready to Publish, Published)
- **Weekly Goals**: Set and track your content goals for the week

### 2. Content Database

Create a database with the following properties:

- **Title**: Name of the content piece
- **Status**: Select (Idea, Researching, Writing, Editing, Scheduled, Published)
- **Platform**: Multi-select (Blog, LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, YouTube, Newsletter)
- **Content Type**: Select (Article, Video, Social Post, Thread, Newsletter)
- **Publish Date**: Date field
- **Priority**: Select (High, Medium, Low)
- **Topic/Category**: Multi-select for content categorization
- **Target Audience**: Text field
- **Keywords/Tags**: Multi-select
- **Word Count/Duration**: Number
- **Performance Metrics**: Number (views, engagement, conversions)

### 3. Weekly Views

#### Calendar View
- Group by: Week
- Display: Publish Date
- Color-code by: Platform or Status

#### Kanban Board View
- Group by: Status
- Sort by: Priority, then Publish Date

#### Table View
- Filter: Current week
- Sort by: Publish Date
- Show all properties for detailed planning

### 4. Content Workflow

**Monday - Planning**
- Review last week's performance
- Brainstorm new content ideas
- Schedule content for the week

**Tuesday-Thursday - Creation**
- Write/create content
- Edit and refine
- Prepare graphics and media

**Friday - Preparation**
- Final reviews
- Schedule posts
- Prepare content for next week

### 5. Templates & Resources Section

- **Post Templates**: Quick templates for different platforms
- **Content Ideas Backlog**: Repository of future content ideas
- **Resource Links**: Links to stock photos, design tools, scheduling platforms
- **Performance Tracking**: Weekly/monthly analytics summary

### 6. Quick Actions

- Button: "Add New Content Idea"
- Button: "Schedule This Week's Content"
- Button: "Review Analytics"
- Button: "Generate Content Report"

## How to Use

1. **Duplicate this template** to your Notion workspace
2. **Customize the database properties** to match your specific platforms and needs
3. **Set up your weekly routine** following the workflow above
4. **Add content ideas** to your backlog as they come to you
5. **Review and adjust** your strategy based on performance metrics

## Pro Tips

- Batch similar tasks (e.g., all writing on one day, all graphics on another)
- Keep a running list of content ideas so you're never stuck
- Use templates for recurring content types to save time
- Set realistic goals - quality over quantity
- Track what performs well and double down on those content types
- Schedule content in advance during high-energy periods

## Integrations

- Connect with Zapier to auto-update when content is published
- Use Make.com to sync with your social media schedulers
- Link to your analytics tools for automatic metric updates
