# CRM Database Template

A comprehensive Notion template for managing customer relationships, tracking interactions, managing leads and opportunities, and monitoring sales pipelines with full contact management.

## Overview

This template enables:
- Centralized contact management
- Lead tracking and nurturing
- Sales opportunity management
- Deal pipeline visualization
- Interaction history logging
- Customer health scoring
- Task and follow-up management
- Revenue forecasting
